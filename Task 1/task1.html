About Us

Our Story

Mahad Art Gallery was founded in 2010 with a passion for bringing exceptional art to the public. What started as a small exhibition space has grown into one of the most respected galleries in the region, showcasing works from both emerging and established artists.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to promoting artistic excellence and making fine art accessible to everyone. Our goal is to create a welcoming space where art lovers, collectors, and curious visitors can discover inspiring works that speak to the human experience.

What We Offer

  • Curated collections of oil paintings, watercolors, and abstract art
  • Monthly exhibitions featuring local and international artists
  • Art workshops and educational programs
  • Private viewings and consultation services
  • Art acquisition and investment guidance
